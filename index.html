Our Story

Where Devotion Meets
Comfort

Nestled in the sacred town of Tarapith, Hotel Dwarkadhish – The Kings' Residency is your ideal home away from home. Situated at Kavi Chandrapur, just a 3-minute walk from the revered Tarapith Temple, we combine devotion, comfort, and affordability under one roof.

Our hotel offers clean, spacious, and well-maintained rooms with modern amenities, including air conditioning, geysers, Smart TVs, and many rooms with private balconies overlooking the river. Whether you are visiting for pilgrimage or leisure, we ensure a restful and peaceful stay at unbeatable prices.
